Intro

Hello! I'm Lutfun Nahar, a creative soul passionate about merging fashion design with modern creativity. I’m currently learning digital art and styling to showcase my ideas and bring a fresh aesthetic to modest yet elegant clothing. My dream? To launch a unique gown brand that empowers elegance while respecting cultural values.

Work

Here’s a glimpse of what I’ve been exploring and building:

  • Digital Fashion Sketches – Designing modest yet elegant gowns using Procreate and Illustrator.
  • Fabric Research – Studying textures, patterns, and materials suitable for local and modest fashion needs.
  • Brand Identity Concepts – Working on names, logos, and visual identity for my future gown brand: Dream Corner.

I believe great design speaks without words—and I strive to tell stories through colors, flow, and form.

About Me

I'm Lutfun Nahar, currently living in Bangladesh. I’m an introvert who loves creating in silence. My interest in fashion stems from a desire to offer something rare: gorgeous gowns that reflect modesty and elegance. I dream of starting my own fashion brand where grace meets tradition.
